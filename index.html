Overview

A dark fantasy built for the ones who move unseen.

ShadowHood is a dark fantasy blockchain game where players become elite assassins, infiltrate dangerous territories, eliminate powerful targets, and collect legendary rewards. Every successful mission raises your reputation, unlocks exclusive gear, and expands your influence across the ShadowHood underworld.
